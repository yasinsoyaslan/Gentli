Summary

  • Gentli stores your habits and check-ins locally on your device.
  • Gentli does not sell your personal information.

Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to the Gentli - Gentle Habit Tracker mobile application (“Gentli”, “we”, “us”).

Data we collect

Habit data (local): Habits you create, schedules, reminders, and check-ins are stored on-device.

Notifications: If you enable reminders, the app schedules notifications on your device using Apple’s notification system.

Purchase data: If you purchase Pro, Apple processes the payment. Gentli may receive purchase/entitlement status to unlock features.

Purchases, subscriptions, and RevenueCat

Gentli may use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and entitlements. RevenueCat may process information such as purchase history, subscription status, and device/app identifiers to provide subscription services.

Learn more:

  • RevenueCat: Privacy Policy
  • Apple: Privacy Policy

How we use data

  • Provide core habit tracking features (on-device).
  • Enable reminders (if you opt in).
  • Verify Pro access and restore purchases.
  • Support and troubleshooting (if you contact us).

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers only when necessary, such as subscription management (e.g., RevenueCat) and Apple’s in-app purchase systems.

Data retention

Habit data remains on your device unless you delete the app or remove your habits inside the app. Purchase records are maintained by Apple and may be referenced for restoring purchases.

Your choices

  • Notifications: You can enable/disable reminders in iOS Settings.
  • Delete data: You can delete habits in the app or uninstall the app to remove local data.
  • Subscriptions: Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID subscription settings.

Children’s privacy

Gentli is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We’ll update the “Last updated” date above.
